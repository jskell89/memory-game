Memory Game

This is a test of your memory. You need to match all the cards on the board. (12 cards 6 matches). If you cannot get all the matches in 14 tries you will lose a life. You are given 3 lives. The game will end when you lose all your lives. To win the game you need to match all cards 3 times and receive 3 high scores. You will then be given your highest and average score. Good Luck!
