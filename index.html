Memory Game

This is a test of your memory. You need to match all the cards on the board. (12 cards 6 matches). If you cannot get all the matches in 12 tries you will lose a life. You are given 3 lives. The game will end in two ways, lose all your lives, or collect 3 scores at or below 12. Good Luck!!
